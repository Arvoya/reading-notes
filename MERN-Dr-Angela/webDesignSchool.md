# Color Theory

When picking colors be aware of the 'mood' from your color palette.

Have it tell a story, and convey a message to the user

## Red

- Love
- Energy
- Intensity

## Yellow

- Joy
- Intellect
- Attention

## Green

- Freshness
- Safety
- Growth

## Blue

- Stability
- Trust
- Serenity

## Purple

- Royalty
- Wealth
- Femininity
